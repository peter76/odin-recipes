Lasagna Recipe

Description:

This is a classic vegetarian lasagna with spinach.

Wherever you can try to use fresh ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 250 gram lasagna pasta leaves
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • pinch red pepper flakes
  • 220 gram fresh spinach, chopped
  • 425 gram ricotta
  • 3/4 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1,5 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1 egg
  • 2,25 cup tomato sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Prepare lasagna leaves if necessary
  2. Preheat oven to 190 deg. C

  3. Spinach mixture:

    1. Fry onion
    2. Add garlic and simmer
    3. Season with red pepper, salt and ground pepper
    4. Add spinach and simmer till tender
    5. Transfer to a strainer and press out liquid
    6. Set aside

  4. Ricotta filling:

    1. In a bowl mix ricotta with 1/2 cup parmesan, 3/4 cup mozzarella and salt and pepper to taste
    2. Stir in the egg
    3. When the spinach has cooled enough mix it in
  5. Spread 1/4 cup tomato sauce in oiled baking dish
  6. Cover with lasagna sheets and top with 1/3 of spinach / ricotta mixture
  7. Sprinkle on 1/4 cup mozzarella and 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  8. Repeat twice and add one more layer off lasagna sheets
  9. Top with remaining tomato sauce, mozzarella and parmesan
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes
  11. Remove foil and bake another 20-30 minutes
